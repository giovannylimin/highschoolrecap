Competitions

Olimpiade
Sains
Nasional

The OSN is an annual science competition for Indonesian students held by the Ministry of Education and Culture. There are three stages of selection, district-level, provincial, and national. The choice of contested subjects for junior high school are mathematics, natural sciences (IPA), and social sciences (IPS)

I majored in natural sciences (IPA) and proceeded to the national level in Palembang, South Sumatra, on 15th-21st May 2016. (scroll left to view more images)

Kuis Kihajar

Kuis Kihajar (Kita Harus Belajar) is an annual competition held by the Pustekkom (Pusat Teknologi Informasi dan Komunikasi Pendidikan dan Kebudayaan) Kemendikbud. There is a provincial selection before the nationals. The contested subjects for senior high school are mathematics, civics, Indonesian, English, physics, biology, chemistry, geography, economics, and sociology.

I went to the national-level in Jakarta twice in 2017 and 2018.
I won the national's first place in 2018. (scroll left to view more images)

Kalfor's
Infographic
Competition

To celebrate World's Wetlands Day, Kalimantan Forest Project (a collaboration between UNDP and Ministry of Environment and Forestry(KLHK)) partnered with Dinas Kehutanan Provinsi Kalimantan Barat to hold an infographic design competition for senior high school students.

I won the second place and went to a three days trip in Jakarta (1st-3rd May 2019) (scroll left to view more images)

Industrial
Engineering
Games

IE Games is the biggest annual industrial engineering competition for Indonesian senior high school students held by Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS). The preliminary round is available online and offline, 30 teams with the highest performance are invited to Surabaya for the semifinal and final round.

I teamed with my classmate and we managed to proceed to Surabaya (5th-9th Febuary 2020). We ranked in the 4th Position. (scroll left to view more images)

OSIS, or also known as the student's council, is a student organization present in schools. OSIS is in charge of organizing school events.

Q&A
Group

Q&A Group is a LINE@-based learning platform in Indonesia. We provides educational content in accordance to Indonesia's junior and senior high school curriculum. Q&A influences more than 500.000 students in Indonesia about the smart use of social media.

I joined the Physics Division as a content creator via the open recruitment (documents screening, written competency test, and interview). I posted physics-related content every Saturday. The posts included physics problems (national exam, IGCSE and SAT, and university entrance exam examples) and solutions, as well as physics articles. (scroll left to view more images)
